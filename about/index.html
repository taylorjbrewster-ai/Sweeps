About Sweeps Vault

An independent tracker for social and sweepstakes casinos — built by a player, for players.

Sweeps Vault exists for one reason: there are now dozens of sweepstakes casinos, and most "review" sites just rank whoever pays them the most. We test these sites with our own money and time, then tell you what we actually found — the generous ones, the slow ones, and the ones to skip.

How the ratings work

Every casino gets a tier and a set of value scores:

  • Tier (S / A / B) — our overall confidence: reputation, redemption reliability, game quality, and how it treats players.
  • Daily Value — how much free Sweeps Coins (SC) you can realistically collect each day without spending.
  • Offers — the strength of welcome bonuses and ongoing sales when you do choose to buy.
  • Min RD / Min Buy / Crypto RD — the minimum SC to redeem, the smallest purchase needed for the welcome offer, and the crypto redemption minimum where it applies.

A Top Rated badge marks our highest-conviction picks. New means it launched recently and is still proving itself.

How we make money

Some "Join" buttons are referral links. If you sign up through one, we may earn a commission — at no extra cost to you, and often with a bonus on your side too. It never changes a casino's tier or score, and casinos we don't recommend stay off the list regardless of payout.

Staying current

Sweepstakes sites change fast — rebrands, new clones, shifting redemption rules. We update the list continually and stamp the date on each page so you know how fresh it is. Offers and figures quoted in our reviews reflect what was advertised at the time of writing; always confirm current terms on the operator's own site before signing up or buying anything.
